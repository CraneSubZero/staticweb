About Uncle Bok's Camp

Uncle Bok’s Camp is one of the camping sites here in Murcia, Negros Occidental. A campsite with an elevation of 2,700 ft above sea level. Located at Prk. Magtahos Brgy. Minoyan, Bacolod, 6129 Negros Occidental. To get here you must Ride a Mambukal-Bacolod bus at Libertad Market, Bacolod City drop off upon Bangga Simaguan (Historical landmark in Murcia, Negros Occidental). Ride a habal habal from Bangga Simaguan going to the drilling site and from the drilling site there will be a 15 mins hike going to Uncle Bok’s Camp.

Activities

  • Experience the magic of the great outdoors by camping under a blanket of stars, surrounded by the tranquil sounds of nature.
  • Immerse yourself in breathtaking scenery during guided nature walks, led by experienced guides who share insights into local flora and fauna.
  • Create unforgettable memories around a roaring bonfire, as you enjoy storytelling, laughter, and the warmth of good company.
  • Discover the joy of bird watching and nature photography, capturing stunning images of the diverse wildlife and scenic landscapes.
